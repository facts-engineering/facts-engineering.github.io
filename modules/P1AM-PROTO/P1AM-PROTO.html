    P1AM-PROTO

    The P1AM-PROTO is a kit that contains everything needed to create your own P1AM shield. It provides a large grid of through holes and strips for easy assembly. It's the perfect platform for adding custom features to your P1AM system.

    • Custom fit stacking headers for shields and P1 Series power supplies

    • Injection molded plastic ProductivityOpen housing and latches

    • ProductivityOpen Overlay which can be easily cut to expose parts (LEDs, connectors, etc.)

    PCB Features

    The P1AM-PROTO PCB is designed to allow for easy perfboard style prototyping. With it you can use a variety of existing add-on boards and standard components.

    • Silkscreen to show component keep out areas for best housing fit

    • Footprint for 18 Pin terminal block receptacle (Right Angle, Through Hole, 3.81 mm Centers) and plug for user I/O connections

    • Front adhesive overlay
    • Two P1AM mounting latches
    • One DIN rail mounting clip
